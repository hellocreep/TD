Budget Join-in Small Group Tour

During our small group tours, you will travel by one of our clean, safe and comfortable buses in the company of other tourists. Why Tibet with Us

It's more than just a trip.

Traveling with us isn't about ticking the boxes. We live in Tibet and can get you off the tourist trails and into the heart of the places we visit. Eat where the Tibetan locals eat, and get close to the people and places that make Tibet come alive. We'll do all the legwork for you so there's more time to explore. And we'll be right there with you to help you create your lifetime Tibet adventure.

Take Advantage of our First-Hand Knowledge

Each year we migrate to Tibet as the nomads migrate to the higher summer pastures. I guess we are now nomads too. So we should know the Holy Land better than any other tour operators. We can offer invaluable advice and suggestions for things to see and do while you are there and you can be confident that your unique itinerary has been created by us who have extensive first-hand knowledge of the places and accommodation it contains.

Reassurance while you are away

While you're away, you'll be in the capable hands of Tibetan specialists. You'll be able to contact and visit our office 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
