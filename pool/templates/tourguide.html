Private Tibet Local Tour Guide & Transfer

You can rely on their first-hand knowledge and boundless enthusiasm.

When touring with Tibetdiscovery.com, you'll have a private tour guide, private driver, and an air-conditioned vehicle escorting you at every destination you travel. This will provide you with maximized personal care, security in Tibet, and flexibility.

Our Private Tour Guide

Perhaps most importantly, all Tibetdiscovery.com tour leaders are 100% Tibetans and with their passion to explore and share in the experience, they're perfectly suited to lead discovery holidays. They're keen to introduce you to the places they call home, and will undoutedly show you a few secret spots along the way.

You will always be greeted punctually on arrival at each destination airport. During the tour, they'll stay at your side all the time to make sure everything goes well as planned.

When changing your itinerary is needed due to unexpected reasons, your private tour guides will be more than accommodating in suggesting alternatives to ensure that you have a wonderful experience.

At your departure, they'll make sure that you've checked in and passed through the security checkpoint.

Our Private Vehicle & Drivers

With Tibet Discovery, you can rest assured that we only use the highest quality vehicles available. We use mainly private vehicles, to ensure we get to our desitinationa as swiftly and smoothly as possible, as well as allowing the freedom and flexibility to stop off en route. Our drivers have many years of driving experience in the areas and very reliable.

To maximize your comfort, we use vehicles with extra space for you and your luggage. Depending on how many are traveling together, we generally use SUVs for 3 people or less, sedans or mini vans (7 seats) for 4 people or less, medium vans (9 seats) for 6 people or less, large vans (11 seats) for 8 people or less, small coaches (19 seats) for 16 people or less, and medium coaches (33-37 seats) for more than 16 people.
