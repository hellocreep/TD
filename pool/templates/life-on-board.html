Life on Board

  • Soft Sleeper Cars

    The most comfortable compartments with doors and 4 soft berths in each offer more privacy and fewer crowds.

  • What to eat on board?

    Simple Western and Chinese food are offered in the Dining car. Also some snacks and drinks are sold by the train attendant with a trolley.
