Anhui Travel Maps - Anhui is an inland province located in Southeast China across the basins of the Yangtze River and Huai River. Anhui has been credited as a top tourist destination in China mainly due to the magnificent Mt. Huangshan (Yellow Mountain) and the well-preserved Xidi and Hongcun Ancient Villages. Both of them are listed as the UNESCO World Heritage Sites. Moreover, Anhui is one of the principal tea producing areas in China and the Huang Shan Mao Feng tea is designated as one of China's ten most famous green teas.
