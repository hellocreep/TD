Tibetdiscovery's Style of Travel

On a Tibet Discovery Tour, you can expect amazing experiences and stay overnight (camping or lodging) next to the scenic spots like Potala Palace, Everest, Jokhang Temple, Mt.Kailash, and many other wonderful locations. We travel in specially customized SUVs and vans, equipped with all the necessary gear - all you have to take along is spending money, personal belongings and a sense of adventure and humor!

  • Your Journey

    Travelling with Tibetdiscovery is a great opportunity not just to see the real Tibet but to see a world of possibilities. We take care of all of the little details, so you can take it all in, find your own path and share in our sense of discovery.

  • Your Choices

    Must-see sights or hidden gems? A Tibetan family visit or religious temple discovery? Hiking to the Everest from Old Tingri or driving directly there from Lhasa? Tibetdiscovery gives you a world of choices so you can create the trip you've always dreamed of taking.

  • Your Free Time

    No journey is complete without some time to relax and reflect and find your own path. That's why our trips are packed with loads of free time and opportunities to either hang with your travel mates or go and discover your own way.

  • Your Travel Mates

    Whether you're traveling solo, with friends or family, for the first time or fiftenth time, you'll come home with plenty of interesting viewpoints and exciting memories to remember for a lifetime.

  • Your Crew - private tour guide & transfer

    Your tour crew (private tour guide & driver) has only one simple goal: to help you have the time of your life. They're not only expert guides who take care of all the little details but also fellow travelers who are dedicated to helping you explore in comfort and confidence.

  • Your Value

    When you travel with Tibetdiscovery, you get more included as part of your tour than going any other way - more must-see sights, more activities & more amazing places to stay. Plus our local expertise means you'll get a truly local experience. But don't just take our word for it. Check out some tour reviews and let fellow travelers tell you in their own words what makes Tibetdiscovery different.
