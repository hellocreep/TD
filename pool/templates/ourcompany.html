Company Profile

Tibetdiscovery.com is the leading online travel service platform operated by Niwei International Tours Co.ltd, based in Lhasa, Tibet, since 1996.

Tibetdiscovery.com (online since 2009) provides online Tibet travel services directly to the end consumers for English speaking people from all over the world.

As the leading local tour operator in Tibet, we have a long-established relationship with the hotels, restaurants, and transportation companies of Tibet, and can provide guaranteed service at a lower price.
