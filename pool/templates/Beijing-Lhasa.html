Flight Schedule from Beijing to Lhasa

There are DAILY flights flying between Beijing and Lhasa with a stopover in Chengdu or Chongqing.

Usually the flights depart Beijing in the morning and arrive in Lhasa in the afternoon with about 5 hours. And the flights back from Lhasa to Beijing always depart in the afternoon and arrive at night.

Train Schedule from Beijing to Lhasa

Every SINGLE day, Train T27 starts from Beijing West Railway Station at 20:09 and arrives Lhasa Railway Station on the third day at 16:00.

Note:

1. We do NOT offer ticket-only service because foreign visitors must book a tour from a travel agency and obtain a Tibet Entry Permit before booking Tibet train ticket, according to the Tibet Government;

2. Because the Tibet train tickets are quite popular, especially in peak seasons (from July to October), please make your enquiry at least 3-6 months before your departure date.
