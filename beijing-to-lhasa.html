Train to Lhasa from Beijing

It is no longer a dream to take a train from Beijing to Lhasa. The 3753 kilometers' railway between Beijing and Lhasa covers 1110 km over the world's highest Qinghai-Tibet Railway. There are daily trains transferring thousands of passengers from Beijing to Lhasa for their lifetime Tibet tour.
